## Understanding 1428

The 1428 compound pattern shows multiplication: 14 × 2 = 28. Foundation energy (4 within 14) combined with partnership (2) doubles into abundance (28 contains the 8 of prosperity). This pattern suggests that building together creates wealth—not just financially, but in all forms of prosperity.

Your collaborative foundations are generating returns. The structure you've created with others isn't just stable—it's productive. Partnership that builds things produces abundant results.
</Section>
