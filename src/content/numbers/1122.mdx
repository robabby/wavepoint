## Understanding 1122

The 1122 compound pattern joins two master numbers: intuition (11) with building mastery (22). This is a rare combination of spiritual insight and practical creation power. The 11 represents awakened awareness and inner knowing, while 22—the Master Builder—brings the ability to manifest visions into reality.

When these two master numbers combine, they signal that your inner knowing is ready to be built into something tangible. The doubling in each half shows amplified potential—what you're sensing intuitively can become structure, system, and lasting achievement.
</Section>
