The sequence 54321 is the complete countdown—the full launch sequence. Beginning with transformation (5) and descending through foundation (4), creativity (3), partnership (2), to the singular point of new beginning (1), this pattern marks pivotal moments when everything aligns for major initiation.

This isn't everyday launch energy—it's the full sequence, suggesting something significant is ready to begin. Your runway is clear.
